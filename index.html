Joffre Lakes

Do I need a pass?

Yes, a free day-use pass required from May-Oct (for ages 12 and up)
(unless you have a overnight camping reservation).


‼️Each person requires a pass.

How many passes are available daily?

500 per day.

When can I book my pass?⏳

2 days in advance starting at 7am- they go fast!


Book Pass here

Can I camp there?🏕️

Yes, but only at the Upper Lake with a backcountry camping reservation.

🚫No camping permitted in Winter.

Do I need a camping reservation?

Yes, you need backcountry camping reservation.

Pass fee: $5 per person per night (6yrs+)


(Reservable dates have not been released for 2025 season yet. Check BC Parks site for updates.)

Where can I park?

🚗 Parking lot at trailhead.

🚙Overflow parking lot on the side of the highway just before the main parking lot.

Is it open year-round?

It may close for Indigenous ceremonies.

(Check BC Parks site for updates.)

Can I bring pets?

🐕 Nope. No pets allowed.

Is there cell service?

📵Limited cell phone coverage.

Water situation?

💦 No potable water. Bring your own or sterilize stream/lake water.

Toilets?

🚽 Pit toilets (with toilet paper) exist in the parking lot.

🧻 No toilet paper at middle/upper lakes. Bring your own!

Can I make a fire?

🔥 Fires are strictly prohibited. 🚫
